I'm Midhun Kumar — a Computer Science student at Monash University and founder of Korvant Australia, where I'm building Sentinel: a compliance and certificate management platform for the Australian hospitality industry.

My interest in software comes from wanting to solve real operational problems. Working at Dorset Gardens Hotel gave me first-hand exposure to the complexity of managing RSA certifications, gaming licence obligations, and AML/CTF compliance across a multi-venue hospitality group. That experience became the foundation for Sentinel, which is now in production and serving clients including JMS Hospitality.

I work across the full stack — Django and Python on the backend, Next.js and TypeScript on the frontend — and I care about building software that is reliable, compliant, and actually used by the people it was designed for. Before Korvant, I co-founded Conduit Management, a digital marketing agency where I built SEO automation tooling and helped clients generate over six figures in leads.

This portfolio covers my projects, work history, and writing. If you want to get in touch, find me on GitHub or LinkedIn.
